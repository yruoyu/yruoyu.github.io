Ruoyu Yin

Hi, I'm Ruoyu Yin. I am a theoretical physicist, currently a postdoctoral researcher in the group of Andreas Dechant at Kyoto University.

My research lies at the meeting point of quantum dynamics and statistical physics. I am mainly interested in:

  • monitored quantum dynamics
  • quantum measurements
  • open quantum systems
  • non-equilibrium statistical physics

Much of my work asks how repeated measurement and dissipation reshape quantum evolution — and how the statistics of when events first happen reveal the physics underneath.
